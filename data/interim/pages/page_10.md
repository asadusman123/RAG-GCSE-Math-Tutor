# Hard Questions

1 [DIAGRAM: Upper horizontal line through A and C with rightward arrow; lower horizontal line F, B, D with rightward arrow (parallel lines). Angle 26° at A inside triangle ABC; tick marks on AB and BC segments showing AC-side equality; line CBE passes through B and continues to E below; angle x° marked at B below the line FD. NOT TO SCALE.]

AC is parallel to FBD, ABC is an isosceles triangle and CBE is a straight line.
Find the value of x.
x = ..............................................

Answer

The interior angles of a triangle sum to 180°, and triangle ABC is isosceles, so angles ABC and ACB will be the same
180° - 26° = 154°
The remaining 154° must be split evenly between the two equal angles ABC and ACB
