Add together all of the angles and make it equal to 360°.
8a + (b + 50) + (4b - 2a) + (3a + 2b) = 360
Remove the brackets.
8a + b + 50 + 4b - 2a + 3a + 2b = 360
Simplify the left hand side by collecting like terms.
Be careful with negative terms.
9a + 7b + 50 = 360
Subtract 50 from both sides.
9a + 7b = 310 [1]
