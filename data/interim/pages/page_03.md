2 Find the interior angle of a regular polygon with 24 sides.

Answer

Method 1
The formula for the interior angle of an n-sided regular polygon is ((n - 2) * 180) / n.
((24 - 2) * 180) / 24 = (22 * 180) / 24 = 3960 / 24 = 165
[1]
165° [1]

Method 2
In any polygon, the total of all the exterior angles is 360°.
In a regular polygon, all exterior angles are equal.
Exterior angle = 360 / 24 = 15
Now use the fact that an interior and exterior angle add up to 180°.
180 - 15 = 165
