2 (a) Show that each interior angle of a regular pentagon is 108°.

Answer

The sum of the angles of a polygon of n sides is given by the formula 180 * (n - 2)
180 * (5 - 2) = 540
[1]
In a regular pentagon, there are 5 angles and they are all the same size
540 / 5 = 108°
[1]
