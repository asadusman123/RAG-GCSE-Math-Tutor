The diagram shows two parallel lines PAQ and SBCT.
AB = AC and angle QAC = 43°.
Find the value of x.
x = .......................................

Answer

Angle ACB is an alternate angle with angle QAC, so angle ACB = angle QAC = 43°
And the triangle is isosceles, so angle ABC = angle ACB = 43°
x = 180 - 2 * 43
[1]
= 94° [1]
