(b) The area of pentagon FGHJK is 73.5 cm2.
The area of pentagon ABCDE is 6 cm2.
Find the ratio perimeter of pentagon FGHJK : perimeter of pentagon ABCDE in its simplest form.
......................... : .........................

Answer

When a shape is enlarged by a scale factor, the lengths are multiplied by the scale factor, and the area is multiplied by the (scale factor)2, also known as the area scale factor
Finding the area scale factor
6 cm2 * Area Scale Factor = 73.5 cm2
Area Scale Factor = 73.5 / 6 = 49 / 4 = 12.25
Therefore the scale factor for the lengths is
Length scale factor = sqrt(49 / 4) = 7 / 2 = 3.5
