3 [DIAGRAM: Triangle with apex B (angle 55°) and base vertex A (angle q°). A straight horizontal line ACruns from A through a point to C on the right. A second straight line BD passes from B down through that point, with angle p° marked above the line AC at the crossing and 48° marked below it toward D. NOT TO SCALE.]

In the diagram, AC and BD are straight lines.
Find the value of p and the value of q.
p = ...................................................
q = ...................................................

Answer

BD is a straight line.
Angles on a straight line add up to 180°.
p + 48 = 180
p = 180 - 48
p = 132° [1]

AC is a straight line.
Angles on a straight line add up to 180°.
Find the unlabelled angle inside the triangle by subtracting your answer for p from 180°.
180 - 132 = 48
